airGR R package website

airGR R package website

airGR and airGRteaching are two R packages which brings into the R software the hydrological modelling tools used and developed at the Catchment Hydrology Research Group of Irstea (France), including the GR rainfall-runoff models and a snowmelt and accumulation model, CemaNeige. Each model core is coded in Fortran to ensure low computational time. The other package functions (i.e. mainly the calibration algorithm and the efficiency criteria calculation) are coded in R.

Developed by IRSTEA  Catchment Hydrology Research Group - Antony, France.
